About Me

My introduction

I am a web developer that interests in creating and discovering everthing cool about modern web applications, including frontend development, backend development, and web design.

My interests in programming starts from the first Pygame project where I used Python to write a Whac-a-mole game. I remember trying each way to achieve the desirable effects and how excited I was when I finally created something that I did not even thought I could do before.

Later I landed my interests in web development and fast-forward to today, I enjoy building fully-functional applications online and have acquired a handful of experience about it.

Along my journey of a web developer, I am constantly looking for ways to improve and always glad to embrace new challenges.
